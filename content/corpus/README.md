# Voice Corpus

This directory holds your best-performing LinkedIn posts. The system reads ALL files here to build your voice profile.

## Purpose

Collect 15-30 of your top posts that represent how you actually sound at your best. These train the system to match your authentic voice, not generic LinkedIn-speak.

## Format

- One file per post, plain text or markdown
- Naming convention: `YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.md`
- Example: `2025-11-03-why-i-stopped-chasing-metrics.md`

## What makes a good corpus entry

- High engagement (comments, reshares, saves) relative to your average
- Written in your natural voice, not performative
- Representative of the style and topics you want to keep producing
- Mix of formats: short punchy posts, longer stories, frameworks, lessons learned

## Minimum corpus size

The voice-matching skills work best with at least **15 posts**. Below that threshold, the system has insufficient signal to distinguish your patterns from generic defaults.

## How to populate

1. Go through your LinkedIn activity and identify your top 15-30 posts
2. Copy the full text of each post into a new file here
3. Name each file using the date it was originally posted
4. Include only the post body, not comments or reactions
