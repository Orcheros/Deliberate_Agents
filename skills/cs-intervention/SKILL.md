# Customer Intervention & Expansion

## Objective

For each flagged account, prepare specific intervention or expansion strategies that the founder or CS team can execute.

## Instructions

### For At-Risk Accounts (Red/Yellow)

1. **Diagnose the root cause**:
   - Is it a product issue? (missing feature, broken flow, poor UX)
   - Is it an onboarding issue? (never activated, stuck at a step)
   - Is it a value issue? (don't see ROI, wrong ICP fit)
   - Is it external? (budget cut, team change, seasonal)

2. **Prepare intervention**:
   - **Outreach draft**: Personalized email from founder acknowledging their situation
   - **Offer**: What can we do? (extended trial, guided session, feature unlock)
   - **Escalation path**: If outreach doesn't work, what's next?
   - **Timeline**: When to act, when to follow up, when to accept churn

### For Expansion-Ready Accounts (Green+)

1. **Identify the upgrade path**:
   - What tier makes sense next?
   - What features would they gain?
   - What's the pricing delta?

2. **Prepare expansion play**:
   - **Usage evidence**: "You've hit the X limit 3 times this month"
   - **Value framing**: What they'd gain, in their language
   - **Timing**: When to present (after a win, before a limit, at renewal)

### For All Accounts

3. **Document playbook entry**:
   - If this scenario is repeatable, document it as a CS playbook
   - Pattern: trigger signal -> assessment -> action -> expected outcome

## Output

Write intervention/expansion strategies per account. Write playbook entries for repeatable patterns. Update assignment status.
