# Facebook Platform Rules

## Hard Limits
- Post text: 63,206 characters (but keep <500 for engagement)
- Link preview: Auto-generated from first link in post
- Images: Up to 10 per post
- Video: Up to 240 minutes
- Poll options: Up to 2 (on Pages)

## Algorithm (2024-2025)
- Meaningful interactions weighted (comments > reactions > shares)
- Content from Groups prioritized over Pages
- "Engagement bait" penalized (share if, tag a friend, etc.)
- Video gets more reach than text-only
- Link posts get less organic reach than native content
- First hour engagement is critical

## Best Practices
- Ask questions that invite genuine responses
- Reply to every comment (boosts distribution)
- Post in relevant Groups (with value, not promo)
- Use link in comments if you want text-only reach
- Native video > YouTube links for reach

## Posting Cadence
- Pages: 1-2 posts per day maximum
- Groups: 2-3 per week (don't dominate)
- Best times: 9-11am, 1-3pm (weekdays), 9-11am (weekends)

## Anti-Patterns
- Engagement bait ("Share if you agree!")
- Link-only posts without commentary
- Cross-posting identical content from LinkedIn
- Overly professional tone (this is Facebook, not a conference)
- Posting only promotional content
