# Facebook Hook Archetypes

## 1. Personal Story Opener
Pattern: "[Specific moment/situation] made me realize [insight]"
Example: "A customer called me at 9pm last Tuesday. What they said changed how I think about support."
Best for: Engagement through curiosity and relatability

## 2. Question First
Pattern: "[Genuine question that invites opinions]"
Example: "What's the one tool you use daily that you'd recommend to anyone in our field?"
Best for: Comments, community building, algorithm boost

## 3. Contrarian Observation
Pattern: "I've noticed [thing that goes against common wisdom]"
Example: "I've noticed that the companies posting the least on social media often have the most loyal customers."
Best for: Discussion, shares, thought leadership

## 4. Behind the Curtain
Pattern: "[Something you'd normally keep private about your work]"
Example: "Here's something founders don't talk about: I rewrote our entire onboarding flow 4 times this year."
Best for: Authenticity, group discussions

## 5. Resource Share
Pattern: "[Specific result I got] using [approach]. Here's the breakdown:"
Example: "Reduced our churn by 23% in one quarter using a system I'll share below."
Best for: Value-driven engagement, saves, shares

## Selection Guide
- Community engagement → Question First
- Thought leadership → Contrarian Observation or Personal Story
- Value delivery → Resource Share or Behind the Curtain
