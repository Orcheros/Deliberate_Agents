# Sales Research

## Objective

Build a comprehensive prospect brief using all available data — CRM records, discovery session content, diagnosis data, and ICP context.

## Instructions

1. **Read prospect/lead data**:
   - CRM contact properties (company, role, industry, engagement history)
   - Lead record (discovery session, diagnosis, ICP segment)
   - Any prior interactions or outreach history

2. **Analyze discovery session context** (if available):
   - What pain points did they articulate?
   - What's their current operational setup?
   - Did they mention client work (Partner signal)?
   - What was their diagnosis severity?
   - What AAAERRR gaps were identified?

3. **Build the prospect brief**:
   - **Company context**: What they do, their stage, team size
   - **Pain signals**: Specific problems they've mentioned or that their ICP segment typically faces
   - **ICP match**: Which segment, confidence level, key indicators
   - **Engagement history**: What they've done in the product, when they last engaged
   - **Talking points**: 3-5 specific, personalized points for outreach
   - **Potential objections**: What might hold them back, with pre-prepared responses

4. **Assess qualification**:
   - ICP fit score (strong / moderate / weak)
   - Engagement signal strength (high / medium / low)
   - Recommended action (immediate outreach / nurture / disqualify)
   - Priority relative to other prospects

## Output

Write the prospect brief to the assignment's output location. Update assignment status.
