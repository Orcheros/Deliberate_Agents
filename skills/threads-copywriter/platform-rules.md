# Threads Platform Rules

## Hard Limits
- 500 characters per post
- Images: Up to 10 per post
- Video: Up to 5 minutes
- Links: Supported but not emphasized
- No hashtag discovery (hashtags are decorative only)

## Platform Culture
- Instagram's text-first spinoff — visual audience going text
- Casual > professional
- Personal > brand
- Short > long
- Reactions > dissertations
- Meta's algorithm favors engagement (replies > likes)

## What Works
- Hot takes and opinions
- Behind-the-scenes real talk
- Questions that invite replies
- Relatable observations about work/life
- Humor and self-deprecation
- Short, punchy posts (under 200 chars often best)

## What Doesn't Work
- Long-form content (save for LinkedIn)
- Heavy advice/how-to (save for Twitter threads)
- Corporate announcements
- Hashtag strategies (they literally don't work here)
- Over-polished content
- Anything that feels like it was "crafted"

## Posting Cadence
- 1-3 posts per day
- Consistency matters more than volume
- Engage in replies as much as posting
