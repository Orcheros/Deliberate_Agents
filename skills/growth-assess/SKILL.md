# Step 1: Growth Assessment

## Objective

Understand the current state of the product's market position, competitive landscape, and growth metrics to inform strategy development.

## Instructions

1. **Read your assignment file** (`.deliberate/assignments/{worktree}.md`):
   - What strategic question or initiative is this assessment for?
   - What specific areas need analysis?

2. **Audit current positioning**:
   - Read the landing page copy, marketing materials, and product descriptions
   - What value proposition is currently being communicated?
   - Who is the implied target audience?
   - What differentiators are being claimed?

3. **Analyze the product**:
   - Read the PRD and feature set to understand what the product actually does
   - Identify the core Jobs-to-be-Done the product serves
   - Map features to user outcomes (not just capabilities)

4. **Assess current funnel** (if data available):
   - Acquisition: Where are users coming from?
   - Activation: What % reach the "aha moment"?
   - Retention: Are users coming back? At what frequency?
   - Revenue: Conversion rates, ARPU, expansion revenue
   - Referral: Any organic growth or word-of-mouth signals?

5. **Competitive landscape**:
   - Identify direct competitors (same job, same approach)
   - Identify indirect competitors (same job, different approach)
   - Identify alternatives (different job, user currently substitutes)
   - Note their positioning, pricing, and apparent ICP

6. **Identify opportunities and gaps**:
   - Underserved segments
   - Positioning white space
   - Channel opportunities
   - Messaging gaps between product capability and market perception

## Output

- Assessment document in `.deliberate/reports/{slug}/growth-assessment.md`
- Key findings and opportunity areas ranked by potential impact

## Transition

Proceed to `/growth-strategy`.
