# X/Twitter Platform Rules & Best Practices

## Hard Limits
- Standard tweet: 280 characters
- Premium tweet: 25,000 characters
- Thread: No official limit (practical max ~25 tweets)
- Images: Up to 4 per tweet
- Video: Up to 2:20 (140 seconds)
- Polls: 2-4 options, 25 chars each

## Algorithm Signals (2024-2025)
- Replies and conversations weighted heavily
- Bookmark = strong signal (higher than like)
- Quote tweets with original thought > plain retweets
- First hour engagement determines reach
- Links in tweets get deprioritized (put in reply instead)

## Posting Cadence
- 2-5 tweets per day for consistent presence
- 1-2 threads per week maximum
- Best times: 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-7pm (audience timezone)
- Space threads 2+ days apart

## Culture Notes
- Authenticity > polish
- Self-deprecating humor works
- Hot takes welcome if substantiated
- "Ratio" culture means bad takes get publicly corrected
- Community notes fact-check viral claims
- Don't delete controversial tweets (own them or clarify)

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do)
- Thread announcements without immediate value ("A thread 🧵")
- Engagement bait ("RT if you agree")
- Screenshot tweets of your own content
- Sub-tweeting (passive-aggressive references)
- Fake vulnerability for engagement
