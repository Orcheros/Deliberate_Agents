# Outreach Preparation

## Objective

Prepare personalized outreach materials that the founder can execute — email drafts, LinkedIn messages, and follow-up sequences.

## Instructions

1. **Read the prospect brief** from the research step

2. **Prepare the outreach sequence** (typically 3 touches):

   ### Touch 1: Initial Outreach
   - Channel: LinkedIn connection request + note, or email
   - Personalization: Reference specific pain point from their discovery session or ICP context
   - CTA: Low-friction (reply, book a call, check out a resource)
   - Length: Under 100 words for LinkedIn, under 150 for email

   ### Touch 2: Follow-Up (3-5 days later)
   - Channel: Same as Touch 1
   - Angle: Different value proposition or social proof
   - Add value: Share a relevant insight or resource
   - Length: Shorter than Touch 1

   ### Touch 3: Final Touch (7-10 days after Touch 2)
   - Channel: Same as Touch 1
   - Tone: Respectful close — acknowledge they're busy, leave the door open
   - Length: 2-3 sentences

3. **Brand voice compliance**:
   - Pass the Energy Test
   - No generic sales copy — every message must reference something specific to the prospect
   - No pressure tactics
   - Founder's personal voice (conversational, direct, empathetic)

4. **Prepare CRM updates**:
   - Suggested deal stage progression
   - Task creation recommendations (follow-up dates, actions)
   - Notes to attach to the contact record

## Output

Write outreach drafts and CRM update recommendations to the assignment output location. Update assignment status.
