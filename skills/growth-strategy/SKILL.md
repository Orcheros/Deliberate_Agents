# Step 2: Strategy Development

## Objective

Develop the strategic frameworks that will guide all downstream marketing and growth execution.

## Instructions

1. **Define positioning**:
   - Category: What market category does the product compete in (or create)?
   - For: Specific ICP segments with detailed profiles
   - Unlike: Named competitors and how we differ
   - We: Core differentiator in one sentence
   - So that: The outcome that matters to the ICP

2. **Build messaging framework**:
   - Primary value proposition (one sentence)
   - Three supporting pillars (feature → benefit → outcome for each)
   - ICP-specific messaging variants (same product, different angle per segment)
   - Objection handling matrix (common pushbacks with responses)
   - Proof points (social proof, metrics, testimonials to source)

3. **Define ICP segments in detail**:
   - For each segment: role, company size, pain points, buying triggers, objections
   - Prioritize segments by TAM, ease of acquisition, and expansion potential
   - Map the buyer journey for each segment

4. **Channel strategy**:
   - Rank channels by expected ROI for this stage and ICP
   - For top 3 channels: specific tactics, resource requirements, timeline
   - Identify one "unfair advantage" channel (where the product has natural distribution)

5. **Growth model**:
   - Primary growth motion: PLG, sales-led, community-led, or hybrid
   - Key activation metric and target
   - Retention target and intervention strategy
   - Expansion/upsell triggers

## Output

- Strategy document in `.deliberate/reports/{slug}/growth-strategy.md`
- Messaging framework document
- ICP profiles
